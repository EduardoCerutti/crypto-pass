Privacy Policy – Crypto Pass

Effective Date: 22/07/2025

Crypto Pass is a utility application designed to help users generate deterministic, encrypted passwords based on a combination of a passphrase and a password input by the user. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about how the app works and how your data is handled.

1. No Data Collection

Crypto Pass does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user input. All operations, including the encryption process, are performed locally on your device.

  • No personal information (e.g., name, email, location, phone number) is collected.
  • No usage analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers are used.
  • No data is stored on our servers — in fact, Crypto Pass does not connect to the internet.

2. Local Encryption Only

All inputs entered into the app (passphrase and password) are used solely for local encryption and password generation on your device. Once you close the app or clear the input fields, the data is lost — nothing is saved.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

Crypto Pass does not share any information with third parties because no information is collected in the first place.

4. Security

The app uses a deterministic encryption algorithm to generate secure passwords based on user inputs. As all processing is done on-device, the security of your data remains fully under your control.

6. Disclaimer on Password Recovery

Crypto Pass does not store any user input or generated passwords. All encryption and password generation is performed locally on the user’s device, and no data is retained once the app is closed.

As such, we are not responsible for any loss of access due to forgotten passphrases or passwords. Users are solely responsible for securely managing and remembering their own credentials.
